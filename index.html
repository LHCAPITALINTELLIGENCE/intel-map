About LH Capital Partners

Built by brokers
who've done it before.

LH Capital Partners is a specialized industrial brokerage firm headquartered in Winter Garden, Florida. We focus exclusively on industrial outdoor storage, small bay warehouse, and net-leased industrial assets — asset classes that require deep specialization and market intelligence to trade effectively.

We are not a generalist firm. Our partners have spent decades building relationships with the owners, operators, and investors who define the Florida industrial market — and we bring that network and expertise to every transaction.

The firm was founded on a simple belief: that a small, specialized team with superior data and deep relationships will consistently outperform larger generalist firms on the deals that matter most.

Why We Don't Publish This

Our comp database is a competitive advantage — for us and for the clients we work with. Putting it on LoopNet or CoStar turns proprietary intelligence into public knowledge and eliminates the edge it provides in negotiations.

We share the relevant slice with owners, buyers, and operators who are actively transacting — not researchers, not competitors, and not people who won't engage seriously with what they see.
